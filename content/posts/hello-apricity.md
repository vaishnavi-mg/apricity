There is a word — *apricity* — for the warmth of the sun in winter.

Not the warmth of summer, which is expected and ordinary. This is the surprise of light on a cold day. The gentleness of the sun when the world has given up on warmth.

That's what this blog is.

## Why write?

I write because some things are too heavy to hold alone, and too personal to say out loud. Words give them a shape. A shape I can look at, turn around, understand.

> *"Writing is the act of reaching across the gap of loneliness."*

I don't know who said that. But I felt it, so I remembered it.

## What this is

This is not a newsletter. There's no schedule, no algorithm, no strategy.

It's just a place where I'll put things when they need somewhere to go.

A warm corner. A little light.

Welcome.
